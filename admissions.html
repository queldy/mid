Admissions

Our admissions process is designed to ensure that motivated students can join and thrive in Innovatech Engineering College.

Application Process

  1. Complete the online registration form.
  2. Submit academic transcripts and documents.
  3. Attend an interview (if required).
  4. Receive acceptance notification.
